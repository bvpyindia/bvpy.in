About BVPY Program

Program Overview

The Bhartiya Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (BVPY) is a comprehensive scholarship program working under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. Our mission is to provide equal opportunity and remove financial barriers for meritorious students.

National Development

BVPY focuses on building a skilled workforce for national development by providing free education to underprivileged children, helping them study alongside others without financial constraints.

Breaking Poverty Cycle

Education equips children with skills and knowledge, helping them secure better jobs and break the cycle of poverty, contributing to economic growth and social development.

Frequently Asked Questions

BVPY (Bhartiya Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana) is a 100% scholarship program under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. Students from families with income not exceeding ‚Çπ1 Lac can apply after qualifying the BVPY Scholarship Exam 2026.

BVPY provides free education with up to 90% scholarship coverage for students in grades 8th, 9th, and 10th. This includes classroom tuitions, foundation preparation, digital resources, and competitive exam preparation.

The BVPY scholarship exam consists of 50 questions for 100 marks with a duration of 1 hour (60 minutes). All questions are compulsory and the exam is available in both online and offline modes.

Required documents include: Student's Aadhar Card, Parents/Guardian Aadhar Card, Father/Guardian PAN Card, Caste/Income Certificate, and 10th Marksheet (if available). All documents should be xerox copies.

Students can choose from PCM (Technology, Engineering, Defence), PCB (Medical, Pharmacy, Life Sciences), or PCMB (both options). Subjects include General Science, Computer Science, IT, Electronics, and Home Science. We also provide preparation for competitive exams like IIT-JEE, NEET, NDA, AIIMS, etc.
